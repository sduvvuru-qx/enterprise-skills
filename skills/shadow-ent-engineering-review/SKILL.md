# shadow-ent-engineering-review

Use when engineering material must be turned into a finished review artifact instead of remaining as raw notes or source files.

## When to Use

- preparing an internal engineering review
- reducing evidence for management or customer discussion
- converting tracker, drawing, and meeting inputs into review bullets
- building slide-ready or document-ready review content

## Workflow

1. Gather evidence:
   - tracker or workbook state
   - drawing or revision evidence
   - meeting or email decisions
   - quality / process notes
2. Reduce evidence:
   - what is proven
   - what is inferred
   - what remains open
3. Choose audience mode:
   - internal working review
   - polished internal leadership update
   - customer-safe summary
4. Rewrite into concise findings and next actions.

## Key Decisions

- **Internal audience**: allow more uncertainty and technical detail.
- **Customer audience**: state only what is supportable and decision-relevant.
- **Evidence conflict**: surface conflict explicitly instead of averaging it away.

## Gotchas

- Dumping raw evidence instead of reducing it.
- Mixing internal working language with customer-safe wording.
- Presenting inference as settled status.

## Output Shape

- finding
- evidence basis
- impact
- status
- next action
