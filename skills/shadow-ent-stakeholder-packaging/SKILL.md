# shadow-ent-stakeholder-packaging

Use when the technical content is correct but the packaging must change by audience.

## When to Use

- stakeholder-sensitive deck or email work
- audience framing
- role-based presentation choices
- deciding what evidence a given audience will trust

## Workflow

1. Identify audience person or role type.
2. Decide what level of detail and evidence they need.
3. Adjust framing while keeping the technical truth unchanged.
4. Preserve the difference between internal and external wording.

## Key Decisions

- **Engineering audience**: more detail, explicit caveats, stronger evidence chains.
- **Leadership audience**: compress to decisions, impacts, and asks.
- **Customer-facing audience**: clean language, evidence-backed status, no internal wandering.

## Output Shape

- audience
- what they care about
- evidence style
- phrasing constraints

## Gotchas

- Changing the underlying technical truth while "tailoring."
- Reusing internal shorthand with external readers.
- Overpersonalizing when only a role-based lens is justified.
